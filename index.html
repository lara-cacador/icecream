Sentimento means feeling in Portuguese. We chose Sentimento because there is an undeniable feeling of happiness when you take that first lick on the hottest day of the year, the feeling of comfort when you are on your couch eating spoonfuls as you watch your favorite movie or even the feeling of sadness when the box comes to an end.

Our ice cream is capable of bringing out a thousand feelings in you all at once! Do you dare to feel it?
